events & weddings

Aretsky’s Patroon is the perfect setting for an unforgettable, undeniably New York event, whether you nestle into one of our intimate rooms for a celebratory dinner or take over our entire townhouse for a wedding or special occasion.

Our six private rooms – each with a unique theme, and featuring an eclectic collection of 20th century photography – lend themselves to memorable events with an upscale air, and can be as laid-back or as refined as you like.
